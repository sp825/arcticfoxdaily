About us

Our two-part rescue mission at Arctic Fox Daily are to provide forever sanctuary to captive-bred unwanted, neglected, abandoned, non-releasable wildlife, specializing in foxes and wolfdogs. And to practice wildlife rehabilitation: We rescue, raise, and release orphaned, New York-native wildlife. The end goal of wildlife. All of our non-releasable permanent residents were either born in captivity - not taken from the wild - or have a permanent disability that would prevent them from surviving and thriving in the wild. As of September 2020, we are officially a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization! This means that members of the public and businesses can now make tax-deductible donations to our rescue.
